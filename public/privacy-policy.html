Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

Ni Bin Guy respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our website, contact us, or submit a booking or enquiry form.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, we are the “data controller” of the personal information you provide to us.

2. What personal information we collect

When you use our website, booking form, contact form, or WhatsApp booking link, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Your address or service location
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Details about the bins or services you request
  • Your preferred booking date or message
  • Any other information you choose to send us

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information, such as health information, religion, political opinions, or financial details, through our website forms.

3. How we use your personal information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Arrange and manage bin cleaning bookings
  • Contact you about your service
  • Send booking confirmations or follow-up messages
  • Keep basic business and accounting records
  • Improve our service and website
  • Deal with complaints, disputes, or legal obligations

4. Our lawful basis for using your information

Under UK GDPR, we must have a lawful reason for using your personal information.

We rely on the following lawful bases:

Contract:
When you submit a booking request, we use your details to provide or arrange the service you asked for.

Legitimate interests:
We may use your details to reply to enquiries, manage customer relationships, prevent misuse of our forms, and improve our business, provided this does not override your rights.

Legal obligation:
We may keep certain records where required for tax, accounting, or legal reasons. UK self-employed business records usually need to be kept for at least 5 years after the 31 January submission deadline, while limited company records are generally kept for 6 years from the end of the relevant financial year.

Consent:
We will only send marketing messages by email or SMS where we have your consent or where the law allows. You can opt out at any time.

5. How long we keep your information

We only keep personal information for as long as necessary.

As a guide:

  • General enquiries: up to 12 months
  • Booking and customer service records: up to 6 years where needed for business, tax, or legal records
  • Marketing preferences: until you unsubscribe or ask us to remove them
  • Website technical/security logs: for a limited period needed to protect the website

We may keep information for longer if required to deal with a legal claim, complaint, accounting requirement, or regulatory obligation.

6. Who we share your information with

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share your information with trusted service providers who help us run the business, such as:

  • Website hosting providers
  • Email delivery providers
  • Booking or customer management tools
  • Payment providers, if used
  • IT support providers
  • Professional advisers, such as accountants or legal advisers
  • Regulators or authorities, if legally required

Where we use third-party service providers, they are only allowed to use your information to provide services to us.

7. International transfers

Some of the services we use may store or process data outside the UK. Where this happens, we will take reasonable steps to ensure your information is protected using appropriate safeguards, such as UK-approved contractual protections or equivalent measures.

8. Cookies and website tracking

Our website may use essential cookies or similar technologies to make the website work properly.

If we use non-essential cookies, such as analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies, we will ask for your consent where required. You can reject or manage non-essential cookies through the cookie options shown on the website.

9. How we protect your information

We take reasonable steps to keep your personal information secure, including:

  • Limiting access to people who need it
  • Using secure website and email systems where possible
  • Keeping devices and accounts protected
  • Avoiding unnecessary collection of data
  • Removing information when it is no longer needed

No website or email system is completely secure, but we take appropriate steps to reduce the risk of loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.

10. Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have rights over your personal information. These may include the right to:

  • Ask for a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information in certain circumstances
  • Ask us to restrict how we use your information
  • Object to certain uses of your information
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on consent
  • Ask for your data to be transferred, where applicable

The ICO lists individual rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and rights relating to automated decision-making.

To use your rights, contact us at: aabincleaning@gmail.com

We may need to confirm your identity before responding.

11. Marketing messages

We will not send you marketing emails or texts unless we have a lawful basis to do so.

You can opt out of marketing at any time by contacting us at: info@nibing.uy

Service messages, such as booking confirmations or appointment updates, are not marketing.

12. Automated decision-making

We do not use your personal information for automated decision-making or profiling that has a legal or significant effect on you.

If you are unhappy with how we use your personal information, please contact us first so we can try to resolve the issue.
