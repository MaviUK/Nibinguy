How much does wheelie bin cleaning cost?

Regular domestic bin cleaning starts from £5 per bin. One-off domestic cleans are available from £15, with commercial prices based on bin size and frequency.

Do you charge extra for first cleans?

No. We do not charge an extra first-clean fee. Your first regular clean is charged at the normal regular-clean price, and one-off domestic cleans start from £15.

What areas do you cover?

Ni Bin Guy is based in Bangor and covers County Down areas including Bangor, Newtownards, Donaghadee, Holywood, Comber, Dundonald, Millisle, Ballywalter, Portaferry and Ballyhalbert.

How often should I clean my bin?

A 4-weekly clean works well for most homes because the bin is cleaned after collection before smells, bacteria and flies build back up. One-off cleans are available when a bin needs a fresh start.

Can bin cleaning help with maggots and bad smells?

Yes. We remove leftover waste where safe, wash the inside and outside, then sanitise and deodorise the bin to help reduce rotten smells, flies, maggots and bacteria.

What bins do you clean?

We clean common domestic wheelie bins including black, blue, brown and green bins, plus larger commercial containers where required.

Do you clean commercial bins?

Yes. We clean domestic wheelie bins and commercial bins, including 360L, 660L and 1100L containers for businesses that need regular bin cleaning.

When is the best time to clean a wheelie bin?

The best time is usually after your bin collection, when the bin is empty and ready to be washed, sanitised and returned fresh for the next cycle.

Do I need to be home for a bin clean?

No. As long as the bin is out and accessible after collection, we can clean it and return it to its usual spot.

What happens if there is loose waste left in the bin?

Where it is safe to do so, leftover waste can be removed before cleaning. Excessive, unsafe or heavy waste may need to be cleared before the bin can be cleaned properly.

How do I book a wheelie bin clean?

Use the Book a Clean button on the website, or contact Ni Bin Guy by WhatsApp, phone or email. We will reply with your price and next available clean date.
