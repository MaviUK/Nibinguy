Questions Customers Ask Before Booking

Looking for wheelie bin cleaning in Bangor, Newtownards, Donaghadee, Holywood or across County Down? Here are the answers people usually need before booking Ni Bin Guy.

How much does wheelie bin cleaning cost?

Regular domestic bin cleaning starts from £5 per bin. One-off domestic cleans are available from £12.50, with commercial prices based on bin size and frequency.

What areas do you cover?

Ni Bin Guy is based in Bangor and covers County Down areas including Bangor, Newtownards, Donaghadee, Holywood, Comber, Dundonald, Millisle, Ballywalter, Portaferry and Ballyhalbert.

How often should I clean my bin?

A 4-weekly clean works well for most homes because the bin is cleaned after collection before smells, bacteria and flies build back up. One-off cleans are available when a bin needs a fresh start.

Can bin cleaning help with maggots?

Yes. We remove leftover waste where safe, wash the inside and outside, then sanitise and deodorise the bin to help reduce rotten smells, flies, maggots and bacteria.

Do you clean commercial bins?

Yes. We clean domestic wheelie bins and commercial bins, including 360L, 660L and 1100L containers for businesses that need regular bin cleaning.

When is the best time to clean a wheelie bin?

The best time is usually after your bin collection, when the bin is empty and ready to be washed, sanitised and returned fresh for the next cycle.
